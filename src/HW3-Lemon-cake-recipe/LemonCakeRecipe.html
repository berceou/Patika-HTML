Lemon Cake Recipe


How about different flavor for special days?

You should definitely try this recipe, which is both easy and delicious. Flavored with lemon zest and juice, and drizzled with a tart lemon glaze, this lemon pound cake is the ultimate dessert for lemon lovers.





Ingredients:

  • 225g unsalted butter
  • 180g caster sugar
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • 65g caster sugar





Recipe for the cake:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Beat together the butter and caster sugar until creamy, then add the eggs, one at a time, slowly mixing through.
  3. Sift in the self-raising flour, then add the lemon zest and mix until well combined.
  4. Line a loaf tin (8 x 21cm) with greaseproof paper, then pour in the mixture and level the top with a spoon.
  5. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes.
